TRAINING

Video games played at a high level require several different mental abilities to be tuned in to the most precise of margins. The most basic of these being your reaction time. Reaction time is a measurement of how quickly you are able to react to certain events occuring in games. An example would be how quickly you are able to react to an enemy apprearing on your screen an then take action.

Below is a way to both test and improve your reaction time. Wait for the box to turn green, then click as fast as possible. Your resulting reaction time will be displayed below the box.
